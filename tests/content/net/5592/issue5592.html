  1. Open the firebug , enable and open the net Panel
  2. Click this button to generate a request
  3. Right click on the new entry in the Net panel and execute Copy URL Parameters
  4. Check the clipboard it should be equal to: 
    v1=d1
v2=d2
  5. Right click on the new entry in the Net panel and execute Copy POST Parameters
  6. Check the clipboard it should be equal to: 
    value1=param1
value2=param2
