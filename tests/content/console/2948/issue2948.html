This test-case is intended for Issue #2948 - Console stops working (No XHR or log outputs).
Jan Odvarko, odvarko@gmail.com

  1. Open Firebug and enable the Console panel.
  2. Check "Show XMLHttpRequests" via the Options Mini Menu
  3. Click on the Execute Test button below and verify that there is a XHR log in the Console.
  4. Click on the Open Popup button and close the opened window.
  5. Click on the Execute Test button and again verify that there is a new XHR log.
