Issue 5454: Scroll to see the props when a group is expanded inside the Computed side panel

Steps to reproduce

  1. Open Firebug
  2. Switch to the HTML panel and there to the Computed side panel
  3. Inspect the blue <div> above
  4. Check the panel option Show User Agent CSS
    ⇒ The panel should display CSS properties and a scrollbar to scroll through them.
  5. Scroll the panel down to the Background category and make sure the whole group is displayed
  6. Click the group header of the Background category
    ⇒ The group should be collapsed now.
  7. Click the group header again
    ⇒ The panel should not be scrolled and the group should be expanded again.
  8. Scroll up again, so that only the header of the Background category is visible
  9. Click the group header of the Background category again to collapse it
  10. Click the group header again
    ⇒ The panel should be scrolled down, so that the whole Background group is visible (aligned at the bottom of the panel).
  11. Scroll the panel, so that just a part of the group header is visible
  12. Click the group header of the Background category again to collapse it
  13. Click the group header again
    ⇒ The panel should be scrolled up, so that the whole Background group is visible (aligned at the top of the panel).
  14. Resize Firebug so that the Background category doesn't fit into the viewport completely
  15. Click the group header it again to collapse it
  16. Click the group header again
    ⇒ The panel should be scrolled down, so that the Background group fills out the whole panel (aligned at the top of the panel).
  17. Expand the font-family property and repeat steps 4 to 13 for it.

Expected result

  • The Computed side panel should smartly scroll when a category or a property is expanded.
