Issue 5639: Allow deleting all watch expressions at once

  1. Open Firebug
  2. Enable and switch to the script panel
  3. Switch to the Watch side panel
  4. Add this watch expression (without the quotes): "a"
  5. Add this watch expression: "b"
  6. Right click on the "a" watch expression and click on "Delete All Watches"
  7. Every watch should be removed
  8. Redo steps 4 and 5
  9. Right click on an empty space of the Watch panel
  10. Click on "Delete All Watches"
  11. Every watch should be removed
